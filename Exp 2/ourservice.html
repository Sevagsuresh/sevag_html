OUR SERVICES

Library/Reading Room
The Library and Information Centre has 35400 Books, 90 Journals and 30 Magazines.


Computer Lab
CS/IT Department have four laboratories with modern systems. Internet lab , DS lab, ICT lab , WAD lab.


Transportation
Transportation facilities are available for the students and staff staying outside the campus via different routes.


Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls
Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls are available in the campus. The hostels are supervised by inmate wardens.


Canteen
A canteen on campus serves students and staff members.
